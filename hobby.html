My Hobbies

GARDENING:

Gardening is my hobby. I derive great pleasure from this hobby. At the back of my house, there is a small plot of land. It is my garden. I grow flowers and some vegetables of the season in this plot. Gardening which I thoroughly enjoy and admire. I have a natural affinity for the natural environment. Outdoor life is unquestionably superior to our urban comforts and luxuries. I like to remain indoors only when the weather is excessively hot or cold. When the weather is nice and pleasant, however, I hurry out to enjoy the fresh air and natural surroundings.

SINGING:

Signing is another favorite hobby of mine. From very little age I have been singing songs and learning music. I was a student of CHAYANAUT SHANGEET BIDYATAN. My favorite form of music is classical, RABINDRA sangeet. Signing gives me mental peace. I feel whenever I am sad, singing gives me relief form sorrowing. While I listen to music, I feel relax and calm. Singing is an excellent way of entertainment. I like to sing because it is fun to sing. I also like to sing because I like to sing with my friends. Singing gives me a great pleasure.t helps me to forget my daily problems. Every person has his own favorite groups or singers. My favorite singers are NACHIKETA, LATA MUNGESHKAR.

TRAVELLING:

Though I don’t travel now that much, but in future I want to travel a lot. Travelling is also my favorite hobby. Travelling is in my hobby list because travelling, I believe, can open a person’s eye. The more a person will see the world the more he will get to know things better. Travelling gives me a lot of pleasure. I have went to JAPAN, INDIA,THAILAND and different places of BANGLADESH.my most favorite destination still now would be Japan.in future I have plan to travel the world as much as I can. My dream destination is Paris, Switzerland and so on.
